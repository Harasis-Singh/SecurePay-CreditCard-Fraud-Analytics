# Business Questions

## Overview

The project aims to answer key business questions related to credit card fraud detection and transaction monitoring. These questions guided the exploratory analysis, feature engineering, SQL business queries, and dashboard development throughout the project.

---

## 1. What is the overall distribution of legitimate and fraudulent transactions?

**Business Purpose**

Understand the class distribution of the dataset and determine the extent of class imbalance before conducting further analysis.

---

## 2. What percentage of all transactions are fraudulent?

**Business Purpose**

Measure the prevalence of fraudulent transactions and establish a baseline fraud rate for business reporting and KPI monitoring.

---

## 3. What is the average transaction amount?

**Business Purpose**

Establish a benchmark for normal transaction behaviour that can be used when comparing legitimate and fraudulent transactions.

---

## 4. How are transactions distributed across different amount categories?

**Business Purpose**

Identify which transaction sizes occur most frequently and understand customer spending patterns.

---

## 5. Which transaction amount categories experience the highest fraud rates?

**Business Purpose**

Determine which transaction sizes present the greatest fraud risk to support targeted monitoring and prevention strategies.

---

## 6. How does the average transaction amount differ between legitimate and fraudulent transactions?

**Business Purpose**

Compare spending behaviour between legitimate customers and fraudulent activities to identify meaningful differences.

---

## 7. What are the highest-value fraudulent transactions?

**Business Purpose**

Identify high-value fraud cases that should receive priority during investigation due to their greater financial impact.

---

## 8. How are fraudulent transactions distributed across spending quartiles?

**Business Purpose**

Analyze fraud concentration across different spending levels to identify high-risk customer segments.

---

## 9. How can fraudulent transactions be prioritized for investigation?

**Business Purpose**

Rank fraudulent transactions based on transaction amount to help investigators focus on cases with the highest potential financial loss.

---

## 10. What key performance indicators (KPIs) best summarize fraud activity?

**Business Purpose**

Generate executive-level metrics that support dashboard reporting and enable stakeholders to monitor fraud trends efficiently.

---

## Project Outcomes

Answering these business questions enabled the project to:

- Quantify the prevalence of credit card fraud.
- Identify high-risk transaction categories.
- Compare fraudulent and legitimate transaction behaviour.
- Prioritize high-value fraud investigations.
- Develop executive KPIs for business reporting.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for fraud monitoring.
